FAQ

How much does this cost?

We charge a concierge fee of $10 per person, to be paid up front by whoever has set up the Nite.

Why do you need a phone number?

We need a phone number to make sure that if there are any cancellations, that we can get ahold of both parties to make them aware of the situation. We promise we will NEVER abuse having your number and NEVER give it to anyone else. We're NOT fucking around here.

How do you match up groups?

Currently, we match all groups up by hand. We carefully look over both sets of interests and activities to make sure that you won't absolutely hate the other group we set you up with.

What sort of events do you do?

We do all sorts of fun things! Try racing big wheels after a 6 pack. Or how about running through 9 holes of Pub Golf. We come up with all sorts of crazy events.
